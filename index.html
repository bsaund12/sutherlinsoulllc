FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes—we regularly cook gluten‑free, dairy‑free, and nut‑free menus. Tell us what you need.

How far in advance should we book?

For larger events, we recommend 4–8 weeks ahead. For smaller gatherings, 2+ weeks is often fine.

Do you provide staff and rentals?

Yes. We can provide servers, bartenders, and coordinate rentals like linens, chafers, and disposables.
